Storytime

Your truth matters. Your voice matters.

What Storytime Really Is

Storytime is a space for real people to share real experiences — the kind that shape you, break you, rebuild you, or change the direction of your life. These stories are often raw, emotional, and deeply personal. You are free to share as much or as little as you feel comfortable with. Nothing is sugar‑coated here.

Your story will not be posted publicly on this website. Instead, it is shared privately with the Ninja Talks team. If selected, you will be invited to join us for a TikTok Live interview where we talk through your journey together.

Storytime is about honesty, healing, connection, and giving people a voice — especially those who have felt unheard.

Rules & Trigger Warnings

⚠️ Trigger Warning: Stories may involve trauma, abuse, mental health struggles, addiction, grief, violence, or other heavy topics. Please take care of yourself while reading or participating.

✔ You may share your story in as much detail as you feel comfortable with.

✔ Stories must be true and personal — this is not a fiction platform.

✔ No graphic or explicit descriptions of violence or self‑harm.

✔ Do not include identifying details of other people without their consent.

✔ Only one story per person may be submitted each week.

✔ Stories are not displayed publicly — they are used only for TikTok Live interviews.
