Awareness • Movement • Faith • Community

Our Mission

Ninja Talks exists to create a safe, honest, and faith-rooted space where real stories are heard, difficult topics are faced, and people feel seen. We focus on neurodiversity, mental health, body wellness, deep life stories, Christian fellowship, and AI music—bringing them together in one community.

We don’t shy away from hard conversations. We believe in awareness, acceptance, healing, and growth—always with respect for our guests, our viewers, and the One who gave us our voices.

Our Story

Ninja Talks started as a simple idea: what if there was a place where people could speak openly about what they’re really going through—without judgment, without pretending, and without being silenced?

From there, it grew into a movement: live conversations, testimonies, worship, and music—broadcast on TikTok and shared across platforms. Every live, every story, every song is part of something bigger: a community that chooses compassion over criticism, understanding over assumptions, and hope over silence.

Our Values

  • Awareness: We shine light on topics many avoid—neurodiversity, mental health, trauma, and more.
  • Acceptance: Every person has a story. We honour that story, even when it’s messy.
  • Faith: We believe in God’s heart for people, and we reflect that in how we speak and listen.
  • Respect: Guests, hosts, and viewers are treated with dignity at all times.
  • Community: We build together—through stories, worship, and creativity.

Community Guidelines

  • Be respectful: No insults, harassment, or hate speech.
  • Respect the guests and host: Anyone attacking or disrespecting them may be muted or blocked.
  • Sensitive content: Some stories are intense and not for sensitive viewers—view with discretion.
  • Music rights: Artists keep full rights to their songs. By submitting, they give permission for their music to be played live and uploaded to Ninja Talks YouTube and social media channels.
  • Zero tolerance for abuse: Toxic behaviour will be removed from the space.
