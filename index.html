Our Mission

Ninja Talks exists to create a safe, honest, and faith-rooted space where real stories are heard, difficult topics are faced, and people feel seen. We focus on neurodiversity, mental health, body wellness, deep life stories, Christian fellowship, and AI music—bringing them together in one community.

Our Story

Ninja Talks began with a simple idea: what if there was a place where people could speak openly about what they’re really going through—without judgment, without pretending, and without being silenced?

From there, it grew into a movement: live conversations, testimonies, worship, and music—broadcast on TikTok and shared across platforms.

Our Values

  • Awareness: We shine light on topics many avoid.
  • Acceptance: Every person has a story worth hearing.
  • Faith: We believe in God’s heart for people.
  • Respect: Guests and viewers are treated with dignity.
  • Community: We build together through stories, worship, and creativity.

Community Guidelines

  • Be respectful: No insults, harassment, or hate speech.
  • Respect the guests and host: Disrespectful users may be muted or blocked.
  • Sensitive content: Some stories may be intense.
  • Music rights: Artists keep full rights to their songs. By submitting, they allow Ninja Talks to play and upload their music.
  • Zero tolerance for abuse: Toxic behaviour is removed immediately.
