AI Music Night

AI Music Night is a platform for artists to showcase their music to a live audience. Every Friday, we play the Top 10 songs submitted throughout the week, and the community votes for their favourites.

All music is played live on TikTok during the Ninja Talks Friday session.

How Submissions Work

  • Artists may submit one song per week.
  • Submissions must include a YouTube or Spotify link.
  • All rights remain with the artist.
  • By submitting, artists give permission for their song to be played live and uploaded to Ninja Talks social media and YouTube.

How Voting Works

  • Each viewer may cast one vote per week.
  • Voting is done through a Google Form.
  • Votes are counted automatically.
  • The leaderboard updates in real time.
