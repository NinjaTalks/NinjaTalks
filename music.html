AI Music Night

AI Music Night is where artists showcase their AI-assisted or AI-inspired music. We listen live, the community votes, and the Top 10 tracks are celebrated.

This night is all about creativity, innovation, and giving artists a platform to shine.

How It Works

  1. Submit your track: Artists send in one AI-assisted or AI-generated song.
  2. We review & queue: Tracks are added to the playlist.
  3. Live listening: Songs are played live on TikTok.
  4. Voting: Viewers vote for their favourite tracks.
  5. Top 10: The highest-voted songs become the weekly Top 10.

Submission Rules

  • One submission per artist.
  • You must own the rights to the track you submit.
  • Clean content only.
  • Rights: Artists keep full rights. By submitting, they allow Ninja Talks to:
    • Play the track live on TikTok
    • Upload it to YouTube
    • Share it on social media

Voting Rules

  • One vote per person.
  • No spam voting or fake accounts.
  • Top 10 is based on community votes.

When It Happens

Every Friday from 8PM to 12AM SAST on TikTok.

Last Week’s Top 10

Update weekly with last week’s Top 10 list.

How to Submit & Vote

Submit: Use the contact page to send your track link and artist name.

Vote: Voting happens live during the TikTok stream.
