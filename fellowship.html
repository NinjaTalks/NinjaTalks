Fellowship Service

This is our Christian live—where we gather for worship, teaching, prayer, and encouragement. It’s a space to breathe, to reconnect with God, and to remember that you’re not alone.

We talk about real life and real faith, not just “perfect” Christianity. We bring our questions, our struggles, and our praise.

What Happens in Fellowship?

  • Praise & worship: Songs that honour God and create space for His presence.
  • Teaching & encouragement: Short messages, scriptures, and reflections.
  • Testimonies: Stories of what God has done in people’s lives.
  • Prayer: Moments to pray for viewers, guests, and the community.

Weekly Fellowship (Live on Sundays, 8PM–10PM SAST)
