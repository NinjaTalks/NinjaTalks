Fellowship Service

This is our Christian live — a space for worship, teaching, prayer, and encouragement. We gather to reconnect with God, support one another, and grow in faith.

We talk about real life and real faith, not just “perfect” Christianity. Everyone is welcome.

What Happens in Fellowship?

  • Praise & Worship: Songs that honour God and create space for His presence.
  • Teaching: Scripture-based messages and encouragement.
  • Testimonies: Stories of what God has done.
  • Prayer: Moments to pray for viewers, guests, and the community.
