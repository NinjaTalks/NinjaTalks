What Fellowship Is

Fellowship is our weekly live gathering on TikTok — a space where we come together in faith, honesty, and unity. Every Sunday from 8 PM to 10 PM SAST, we read scripture, share testimonies, worship together, and talk openly about life, struggles, healing, and God’s presence in our journeys.
