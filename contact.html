Before You Reach Out

Many people who contact Ninja Talks share heavy, emotional, or personal experiences. You are welcome to share what you feel comfortable with — but please remember:

✔ We are here to listen, not to judge.

✔ We cannot offer medical, psychological, or crisis support.

✔ If you are in danger or struggling severely, please reach out to someone close to you or a local support line.

Your safety matters.
